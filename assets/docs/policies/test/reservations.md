# Equipment Reservation System

To ensure fair access to our equipment, we use a reservation system for most machines.

## Booking Requirements

### Prerequisites
- Valid Fablab membership
- Completed safety training
- Machine-specific certification (where required)
- Project proposal for long bookings (>4 hours)

### Advance Booking
- **General users**: Up to 1 week in advance
- **Members**: Up to 2 weeks in advance
- **Students**: Up to 3 days in advance
- **Classes/workshops**: Up to 1 month in advance

## How to Make a Reservation

1. **Log into** the booking system at [booking.fablab.com](https://booking.fablab.com)
2. **Select your equipment** from the available options
3. **Choose date and time** slot
4. **Provide project details** including:
   - Brief project description
   - Materials you'll be using
   - Estimated completion time
5. **Confirm booking** and save confirmation email

## Equipment Categories

### Walk-in Available
- Hand tools
- Basic 3D printers (PLA only)
- Electronics prototyping
- Computer workstations

### Reservation Required
- Laser cutters
- CNC machines
- Advanced 3D printers
- Resin printers
- Large format printing

### Staff-Assisted Only
- Industrial equipment
- Hazardous material processing
- New/complex machines
- Calibration procedures

## Cancellation Policy

### Advance Cancellation
- **24+ hours**: Full credit, no penalty
- **2-24 hours**: 50% credit
- **< 2 hours**: No credit

### No-Show Policy
- First no-show: Warning
- Second no-show: 1-week booking restriction
- Third no-show: 1-month booking restriction

## Time Limits

### Daily Limits
- **Laser cutters**: 4 hours maximum
- **3D printers**: 8 hours maximum  
- **CNC machines**: 6 hours maximum
- **Electronics**: No limit during open hours

### Weekly Limits
- **Individual users**: 20 hours total
- **Student projects**: 30 hours total
- **Commercial use**: Unlimited (premium rates)

## Extending Your Session

If you need more time:
1. Check availability in booking system
2. Ask staff if extension is possible
3. Be prepared to yield to next user
4. Clean up if you can't extend

## Equipment Etiquette

### Before Your Session
- Arrive on time
- Bring all necessary materials
- Have your design files ready
- Check equipment status

### During Your Session
- Keep work area clean
- Don't leave equipment unattended
- Ask for help if problems arise
- Respect noise levels

### After Your Session
- Clean up thoroughly
- Return tools to proper locations
- Report any issues or damage
- Complete usage log
