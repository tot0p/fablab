# Safety Guidelines

Safety is our top priority at the Fablab. Please read and follow these guidelines at all times.

## General Safety Rules

### Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- **Safety glasses** are required when using power tools
- **Closed-toe shoes** must be worn at all times
- **Long hair** must be tied back when using rotating machinery
- **Loose clothing** should be avoided near moving equipment

### Workshop Behavior
- Never rush or work when tired
- Ask for help if you're unsure about anything
- Keep work areas clean and organized
- Report any accidents or near-misses immediately

## Emergency Procedures

### In Case of Emergency
1. **Stop** what you're doing immediately
2. **Alert** others in the area
3. **Call** emergency services if needed (911)
4. **Report** the incident to Fablab staff

### Emergency Equipment Locations
- **Fire extinguishers**: Near each major equipment area
- **First aid kit**: At the main desk
- **Emergency stop buttons**: On all CNC machines
- **Eye wash station**: In the chemistry area

## Machine-Specific Safety

Each piece of equipment has its own safety requirements. You must be trained and certified before operating:

- [3D Printers](/docs/tools/3d-printers/)
- [Laser Cutters](/docs/tools/laser-cutters/)
- [CNC Machines](/docs/tools/cnc-machines/)
- [Electronics Station](/docs/tools/electronics/)

Remember: When in doubt, ask for help!
